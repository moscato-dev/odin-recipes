Description

Time to cook: About 30-45 minutes including preparation

It takes a bit of effort to make, but is worth it! Flavorful and satiating!

Ingredients

Seasoning

  • .25 cup of paprika
  • 2 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp of onion powder
  • 1 tbsp of ground mustard
  • 1 tbsp of ground thyme
  • 1 tbsp of black pepper
  • A heavy handful of salt

Wet dredge

  • 8 egg whites
  • 4 cups buttermilk
  • A little seasoning

Dry dredge

  • 6 cups of flour
  • .25 cup of buttermilk
  • A good amount of seasoning

Steps

  1. Mix all of seasoning ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Mix the wet dredge ingredients in another bowl.
  3. Mix the dry dredge ingredients in another bowl.
  4. Cover the chicken tenders in seasoning.
  5. Put chicken tenders through the dredges in this order: wet, dry, wet, dry.
  6. Fry it in a pot of cooking oil until it's crispy.
  7. Plate up, enjoy!
