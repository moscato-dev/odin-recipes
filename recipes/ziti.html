Description

This ziti recipe is cheesy, saucy, tasty!

Time to cook: About 1 hour including preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • .5 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ziti
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can of peeled plum tomatoes
  • 4 0z parmigiano
  • 15 oz full fat ricotta
  • 14 oz mozzarella

Steps

  1. Smash the sausage to look like the ground beef.
  2. Cook ground beef and italian sausage in a skillet.
  3. Cook ziti to al dentge.
  4. In a cooking tray, make two layers using halfs of ziti, sauce, meat, and cheese at a time.
  5. Bake it in an oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for about 45 minutes.
  6. Plate up and enjoy!
