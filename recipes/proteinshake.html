Description

This chocolate protein shake is delicious, easy to make, and filling.

It is great for those needing to supplement some extra protein intake.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup of milk
  • .75 cup of banana
  • 1 tbsp of peanut butter
  • A splash of cacao powder or cinnamon
  • 2 cups of ice
  • .25 cup of greek yogurt
  • 1 or 2 scoops of chocolate protein powder

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients listed above.
  2. Combine all ingredients into a blender.
  3. Blend it until it looks smooth.
  4. Pour it out and enjoy!
